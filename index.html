What's up with the 3rd Monday?

  • Martin Luther King's Birthday is actually January 15th
  • George Washington's Birthday is on February 22nd
  • Memorial Day was initially celebrated May 30th

That all changed in 1971 when the Uniform Monday Holiday Act was signed into law.

The rationale came from the appeal of a three day holiday that allowed time for travel and reduced absenteeism for midweek holidays.
